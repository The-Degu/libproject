get to know our team!


Here at the Fumus Library we pride ourselves on having a diverse team. With our team comprising of many people from many walks of life, we encourage them to tell their stories as much as we encourage them to read the ones we keep here. Here are Snippets from our team leads, and keep your eyes open for stories from our whole team in future updates.



Mauve Coronet

Founder and proprietor of the Fumus Library

they/them

Mx. Coronet founded the Fumus Library in 1945. After their personal collection of books became too large for them to keep in their smaller personal Library. They quickly found that sharing and trading their books it's more satisfying than hoarding all of the knowledge to themself.
