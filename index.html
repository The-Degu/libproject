Three letters from Alexander Robluf

This is a series of letters to one Zovot Crown from the legendary Alexander Rublif. The letters denote Mr. Robluf's time in acidemia while doing his thesis work. It is currently unknown who Mx. Crown is. Because they do not appear in any other contemporary literature or in Mr.Robluf's autobiography, but by the writings it appears that the two were close for some time.



Death of a Bloodline

This booklet was found in the stacks two years ago, though it is unknown when it was left. It is the personal story of one Marcus Bennett, his death, and rebirth. Along with his personal relationships with his wife Emilia Bennett and Madam Ruth of the west coast vampire clan.



Magic Diseases and You

the Basics, Facts, and Common Myths. This is a booklet produced by the nonprofit AfMD in 1982. This booklet has basic information surrounding three magical diseases, how they affect the sufferer and common myths surrounding the diseases. While some of the information is dated, the limited-addition booklet itself was donated to our collection and we choose to show it as a piece of history.



About us

The Fumus Library is a semi-private collection of books, novels, letters, and unpublished works where people are encouraged to come and share their stories. The collection is owned by Mx. Mauve Coronet and spans 500 years of literature: from play books to VHS recordings. We buy, sell, and trade rare books but anyone is free to come in and look at the collection. Our mission statement is not to hoard knowledge but to share it freely. . This is why this website was created- to share the words of people whose voices may not have been heard before.
