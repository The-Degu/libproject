Three letters from Alexander Rublif

This is a series of letters to one Zovot Crown from the legendary Alexander Rublif. The letters denote Mr. Rublif’s time in acidemia while doing his thesis work. It is currently unknown who Mx. Crown may be due to their lack of existence in any other contemporary literature or in Mr.Rublif’s autobiography, but by the writings it appears that the two were for some time close.
